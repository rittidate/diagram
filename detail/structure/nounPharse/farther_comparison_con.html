Farther / Further

Expressing comparative distance

FARTHER FURTHER

Farther is used with the meaning of comparative distance. Some speakers prefer farther for physical distance. Others use it for time and figurative distance as well. That is, they use farther and further interchangeably.

Further is preferred by some speakers for figurative distance, for example, "further from the truth". The two words have begun to differentiate.Additional studies are needed to determine their usage in various English communities.

More, additional, extra

FARTHER FURTHER

Farther is not used with the meaning of "more", "additional", or "extra".As a verb, it is used with the meaning of " help" or "aid".

Further is used with a meaning of "more", "additional" "extended" or "extra".
